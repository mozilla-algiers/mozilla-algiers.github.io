About Us

A group of students from the U.S.T.H.B university, we have computer science students,biologists,electonitians and others. We share the same passion, which is to keep the web open and teach the algerian community about Mozilla and it's Mission. we also encourage and help our members to get started and contribute to mozilla projects and other open source projects. If you are curious and want to ask a specific question or suggest something feel free to contact us. Or tweet to us and we'll be more than happy to answer your question and give further informations.

Events

We try to be active as possible as we can, by organizing events and regular meetups about web related topics such as JavaScript, PHP, Firefox and others..
